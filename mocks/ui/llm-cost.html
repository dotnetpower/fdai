LLM cost

Where the model budget is going. T0 is free (deterministic), T1 embeddings are cheap, and T2 reasoning is the expensive minority - the design target is to keep the T2 share small (~5-10% of events). Every dollar is attributed to a tier and a model, so an unexpected shift shows up here before it shows up on the invoice.

Cost is a soft guardrail, not a kill switch. When a tier passes 80% of its daily budget, Njord raises a cost anomaly and Forseti tightens the T2 confidence threshold. The gate never silently drops requests to save money.
