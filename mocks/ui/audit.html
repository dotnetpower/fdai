Guarantees

What the audit log promises and what it does not.

Append-only, ordered

Entries mutating one resource are serialized on a per-resource key. Concurrent actions on the same resource are mutually excluded.

Idempotent replay

Re-delivery uses the idempotency key; a retried action never double-applies. Replay is judge-only and never re-executes.

Secrets never here

No connection strings, tokens, tenant / subscription / resource ids. Actor identity is by role, not by principal-id.

Rollback linked

Every enforce entry references its rollback path (pr_revert, scripted, pitr, snapshot_restore, or state_forward_only).
