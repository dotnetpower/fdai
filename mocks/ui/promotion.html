What the gate checks

All four must be green at the same scenario-set version. A single amber demotes.

Accuracy against reference

Shadow verdict matches the pinned reference agent on the frozen scenario set (per ActionType threshold, usually ≥ 95%).

Zero policy-violation escapes

Any shadow verdict that would have violated a Rego policy blocks promotion outright - not softenable by average accuracy.

Guard metrics within budget

Change failure rate, false-positive rate, and rollback rate must not exceed the baseline plus their alert budget on the same window.

Safety invariants complete

The ActionType declares its stop-condition, rollback contract, blast-radius cap, and audit hook - and each has a passing test.

Automatic demotion

Regression in enforce demotes back to shadow without a human step.

detect regression → demote to shadow → audit entry (with cause) → re-open promotion candidate

Cause of demotion is one of: guard-metric breach, policy-violation escape, or a per-ActionType stop-condition trigger. The audit entry links the offending event.
