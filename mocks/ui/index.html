Design principles

The kit encodes the app-shape rules, not just a look.

Read-only surface

The console visualizes state and the HIL queue. It never executes actions; approvals flow through ChatOps or a remediation PR.

Calm, desaturated

No primary/neon colors and no top/bottom color bands. Muted accents carry meaning (severity, tier, trend) without shouting.
