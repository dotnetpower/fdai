Pantheon

The 15 named agents that own the runtime control plane. Grouped by layer: governance sets direction and remembers, pipeline senses, judges, executes, approves, audits, and domain specialists advise but never execute. Names, ownership, and reporting lines are fork-locked - a fork tunes bindings, it does not add, remove, or rename an agent.

Read-only view. This page renders the pantheon spec that ships with the control plane. Every card is a projection of PANTHEON_SPECS in src/fdai/agents/_framework/pantheon.py.
